What is mean by AI ? Explained Simply

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is when computers are programmed to think and learn like humans, helping with tasks like recognizing speech or making decisions.

It refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, understanding natural language, and more. AI can be divided into various subfields, including machine learning, natural language processing, robotics, and computer vision, among others.

AI systems can range from simple algorithms that automate specific tasks to complex neural networks that can learn and adapt over time. The goal of AI is to create machines that can function intelligently and independently, often to assist or augment human capabilities.
